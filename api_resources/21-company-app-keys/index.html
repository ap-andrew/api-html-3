Developer and App Administration

Company App Keys

/companies/{companyName}/apps/{appName}/keys
The consumer key (or app key) provisioned to a company app
Approve a Company App KeyDetail 
/companies/{companyName}/apps/{appName}/keys/{consumerkey}?action=approve
Verb:POST
Description:Changes state of Consumer Key provisioned to Company App to 'Approved'
Auth:Basic Auth
Delete a Company App KeyDetail 
/companies/{companyName}/apps/{appName}/keys/{consumerKey}
Verb:DELETE
Description:Deletes a company app key
Auth:Basic Auth
Get a specific Key of Company AppDetail 
/companies/{companyName}/apps/{appName}/keys/{consumerKey}
Verb:GET
Description:Get a specific key of a company app
Auth:Basic Auth
Revoke a Company App KeyDetail 
companies/{companyName}/apps/{appName}/keys/{consumerkey}?action=revoke
Verb:POST
Description:Revokes the App Key of a Company App
Auth:Basic Auth
